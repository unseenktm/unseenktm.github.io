About Us

unseenKTM is a digital photo magazine dedicated to showcasing the diverse beauty of Nepal and celebrating the creativity of Nepali photographers. While it began as a platform to spotlight Kathmandu’s hidden gems, our vision has since expanded to embrace the rich visual stories unfolding across the entire country and beyond.

Our magazine proudly features not only photographs of Nepal but also the remarkable work of Nepali photographers from around the world, capturing moments and stories wherever they may be.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to capture vibrant culture, landscapes, and people, ideas and stories through compelling photography. Each issue is a thoughtfully curated collection of images that brings to life the untold stories of everyday moments, fleeting emotions, and remarkable encounters.

unseenKTM is more than just a magazine ‑ it’s a labor of love. Born from a passion for photography, this project thrives on the dedication of volunteers. No one gets paid; every contribution is a testament to the desire to create and connect.

We believe great stories deserve to be seen. Whether you're a seasoned photographer or someone with a unique perspective to share, we invite you to collaborate with us or submit your work. Please contact us for collaborations and ideas. You can reach us at mail@unseenktm.com

How to Submit Your Work:

  • Send us images of your photo project.
  • Include a description of the project, offering insights into the story behind each photograph.
  • Email your submission to mail@unseenktm.com.

Your vision is what makes photography a powerful form of expression and we can't wait to be inspired by your brilliance.
